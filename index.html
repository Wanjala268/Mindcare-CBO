About MindCare Foundation

Founded in 2019, MindCare Foundation emerged from a deep understanding of the mental health challenges facing communities in Nairobi and across Kenya. We believe that mental health is a fundamental human right, not a privilege.

Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to break down barriers to mental health care, providing culturally sensitive and affordable services to those who need them most.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and culturally appropriate mental health services while building community capacity for sustainable wellness.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every individual has access to quality mental health support and communities are empowered to care for one another.
